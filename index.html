About the Event

What is Umoja?

Umoja is YIS's celebration of the incredible cultural diversity within our school community. Inspired by the spirit of Culturama — a beloved tradition at UWCSEA in Singapore — Umoja brings that same magic to Yokohama.


Through dance, music, storytelling, and performance, students take the audience on a journey across continents and cultures — not as observers, but as participants. Every act is student-led, student-driven, and deeply personal.


It is a vibrant tapestry woven from the unique threads of every nationality, language, and tradition represented at YIS — a reminder that our differences are not just welcome here, they are celebrated.

Our Foundation

Why Umoja at YIS?

At YIS, everything we do is guided by three powerful words: Live. Learn. Lead. Umoja is these values in action — a living, breathing expression of what it means to be part of this community.

Live

Umoja celebrates individuality and belonging. It gives every student a stage to share who they are, where they come from, and what makes them unique — fully alive in their own story.

Learn

Watching and performing in Umoja opens windows to cultures we may never have encountered. Every act is an opportunity to deepen our understanding of the world and of each other.

Lead

Students don't just perform — they organise, choreograph, direct, and inspire. Umoja is a leadership experience from the first rehearsal to the final bow.
